Capabilities

01 — Sniper
Launch Sniper

Detects new liquidity pairs on Solana and Base the moment they appear and executes your buy before most traders see it listed. Speed measured in milliseconds.

02 — Migration
Pump.fun Migration Detector

Helius WebSocket listener catches Pump.fun → Raydium migrations in 100-500ms — before DexScreener even indexes them. The earliest possible entry point.

03 — Intelligence
Conviction Scoring

Every token scores 0-100 across 8 dimensions before execution. Two modes — Safety for standard snipes, Momentum for fresh migrations. No other bot does this.

Dev Wallet Tracker

The only sniper that watches after you're in. Identifies the dev wallet, monitors their balance every 30 seconds, and alerts — or auto-sells — the moment they move.

Uses postTokenBalances — not signer detection — to catch coordinated same-block wallet distributions. Catches bundled launches that slip past every other filter.

Set your exit before you enter. Alien Intelligence closes positions at your target without emotion, without delay, without needing you awake.

08 — History
Trade Intelligence

Win rate, avg ROI, best trade, worst trade — all logged per snipe with conviction score. Most bots just execute. Alien Intelligence helps you learn.
